More than a blockchain game

When you buy a virtual tree, we plant a physical tree in the location of your choice from the available pool. Each tree generates AIR tokens which represent how much you're helping the environment with clean air. You can see the impact you're making to the world with a real-time dashboard that gets data straight from your trees using IOT devices powered by arduino.

Trees capture carbon dioxide to generate breathable oxygen that cleans the polluted air. They also convert bad smells into neutral or pleasant fragances naturally generated which massively improves the quality of life of people in big cities while saving the world.

Our Process

  • 1. Buy a collectible tree

    We created a live decentralized game that allow people from all over the globe to create their own forest in which they can purchase trees and see their growth as time goes by using real-time data straight from your trees.

  • 2. We plant a physical tree

    Each tree you purchase, is a new tree that's maintained by our team members so that it flourishes beautifully. You have a wide variety of trees to choose from and you don't have to worry about maintainance, it's all on us. A small monthly fee will be required to keep the trees in perfect condition as management expenses.

  • 3. Trees generate AIR tokens daily

    Weekly, as your tree generates clean oxygen, you get rewarded AIR tokens that can be used for a variety of purposes. The more trees you have and the older they are, the more AIR you generate. AIR tokens are representative of how much oxygen has been generated from reducing carbon dioxide.

  • 4. Spend AIR tokens for rewards

    Once you have enough AIR, you can head out to our shop which provides you with discounts, coupons and all sorts of tradable objects that you can use immediately. You can also sell your AIR for fiat or crypto if you wish so at any point. Alternatively, you can use your AIR for management costs related to keeping your trees in perfect condition.

  • 5. Trade trees with other members

    If you get tired or want to change your tree portfolio, you can sell and buy trees from other members without any additional fees so that you have a wider variety of plants.

Here's why you should care

  • 6.5 million people die each year because of diseases related to air pollution.
  • Up to 40 IQ points are lost by children that live in the most polluted areas.
  • 92% of the population lives in places where air pollution exceeds safe limits.
  • China is the most polluting country in the planet where a third of all global air contamination related deaths take place.
  • 64% of lakes are not clean enough for fishing and swimming because of bacteria, mercury, phosphorus and nitrogen produced by contamination.

We are working on improving the efforts taken to fight air contamination with a simple action: planting trees. Trees are the lungs of the planet and they are our most efficient tools for reducing air and smell pollution.
